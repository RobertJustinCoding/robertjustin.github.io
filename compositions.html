Title: Carson Case

Author: RJ

Story Description: When murders begin to be of one surname, a college student voluntarily took the lead in the study, aiming to forcefully reveal the nemesis' identity. (LOG LINE)

Continuous cases have been flooding lately- only one thing in common, their 'surnames'. Intrigued by undisclosed things, Siobhan Annesley, a college student, soon made it a habit to explore and analyze random things. It even became the backbone of the decision of her college course-criminal justice! Yet it never stops there; Siobhan, now wanted to join an organization to kill off some of her time. (BLURB)
